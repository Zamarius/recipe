ULTIMATE RECIPE

Step 1: Cook the French fries per package instructions

Step 2: Put the French fries on an oven safe serving dish and add cajun seasoning

Step 3: Add cheddar cheese and bacon ; melt the cheese

Step 4: Mix Sour Cream and Ranch for dipping sauce

Step 5: Serve hot
